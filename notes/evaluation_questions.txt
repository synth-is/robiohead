The IMS offers navigation of a just intonation tuning lattice by tilting it. Furthermore the navigation can be based on single note playback or chords composed of tetromino forms; different forms can be selected, to obtain different note selections for the chords, rendering different harmonies. Sounds Can be change by a "hidden" gesture / affordance.


Learnability / explorability:

Can you discover and navigate any interesting chord progressions by tilting the (fusion pose) thing?

Do you find that you develop skill in navigating the chords / notes as you practise?
- can you instantly make some music while gradually discovering / mastering some subtleties?


Controllability:

Can you observe the effect of selecting the different tetromino forms for navigating the tuning lattice? Did you land on any favorite settings?

Did you discover the hidden affordance for changing sounds?
